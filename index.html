Master Your
Choreography Routine

Whether you’re an advanced dancer or just starting out, learning new choreography can be an exciting challenge. Koreo enhances the choreography-learning experience by incorporating both a timeline and the social aspect of dance, while boosting memorization. Find new choreography to master or or share routines with your dance team. Feel confident when it’s time to dance and perform.

Key Features

Create

Create detailed choreography with timestamps

Mark

Use markers to go back to any point in the choreography

Share

Share with your dance team, with other users, or keep your choreography private

Compare

Record your progress and view side-by-side with original video to see improvement
